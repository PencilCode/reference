Arcs can be drawn by adding a second arguement to the familiar [lt](lt.html) and [rt](rt.html) commands. 

###Syntax

<pre class="jumbo">
lt <span data-dfn="angle">135</span>, <span data-dfn="radius">60</span>
</pre>
